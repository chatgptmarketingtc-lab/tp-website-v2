Questions?

Answers, up front.

Still curious? Email hello@tatva.health — a real human replies within a working day.

Will my handwriting actually work?

Yes. SmartPad is trained on Indian clinical handwriting — including the illegible kind. Most doctors reach 95%+ recognition inside a week.

Does VoiceRx work in my regional language?

English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Bengali, Gujarati, Malayalam — including code-switching between any two mid-sentence.

Where is my patient data stored?

In Microsoft Azure's Mumbai and Pune regions, encrypted at rest. Audio for VoiceRx is processed in-region and never used to train shared models.

Can I move my existing records in?

Yes. We migrate from most major Indian EMRs, paper registers, and Excel sheets. A migration engineer handles it during your onboarding week.

What happens if the internet drops?

Tatva runs offline. Notes queue locally and sync silently the moment you're back online.

Is Tatva ABDM certified?

Yes, at Milestone 2. Patients can link their Health ID and carry their records across any ABDM-enabled facility.
