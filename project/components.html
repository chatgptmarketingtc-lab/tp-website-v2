FAQ
Does VoiceRx work in Indian languages?+

Yes. Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Kannada, and Gujarati are supported today. More on the roadmap.

How does ABDM compliance work?+

Prescriptions and records are emitted in FHIR R4 with ABHA linking. We're an ABDM-empanelled HIE-CM partner.

Can I keep using my paper Rx pad?+

Yes — SnapRx photographs your handwritten Rx and digitises it. SmartSync syncs writing on a tablet in real time.
