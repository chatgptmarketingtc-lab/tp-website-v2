About Us

Software for how Indian doctors actually work.

Tatva is an AI-native EMR built around real OPD constraints: multiple languages, intermittent internet, paper habits, and a genuinely crowded consult room.

"The best software for doctors disappears."

Tatva began in 2021 after our co-founder watched her father, a 30-year internist, spend two hours a night catching up on electronic charts.

Today 10,000+ clinicians across India trust Tatva with their daily OPD. We are still a small team, obsessed with the same problem.
