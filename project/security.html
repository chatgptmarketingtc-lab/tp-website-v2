How we secure your data

Three pillars. Zero shortcuts.

🔐

Encryption everywhere

AES-256 at rest. TLS 1.3 in transit. Per-tenant key isolation. Patient data is encrypted at every layer of the stack — on device, on the wire, and in storage.

🧑

Role-based access & audit logs

Granular roles (doctor, nurse, receptionist, admin) with field-level permissions. Every read and write logged with user, time, IP, and reason. Logs are immutable and exportable for audits.

🇮🇳

India data residency

All patient data stored exclusively in India-resident regions on tier-3+ infrastructure. Your data never leaves India unless you explicitly enable cross-border replication.
